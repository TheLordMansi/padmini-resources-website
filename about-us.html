About Us

A trading house built on repeat containers

Padmini Resources Indo Africa SARL was founded in 2012 to connect Indian farms and mills with importers who need consistent quality, honest grading and paperwork that clears customs the first time.

We trade two lines: cold-chain fresh produce from Maharashtra's grape, pomegranate, mango and onion belts, and staples — rice, pulses, spices, tea — sourced from established mills and processors across India.

Our philosophy is plain: quote what we can ship, grade what we quote, and document everything. That is why most of our volume is repeat business.
