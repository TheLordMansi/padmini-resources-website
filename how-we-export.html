Payment Terms

Standard instruments, no surprises

LC AT SIGHT

Letter of Credit

Your bank releases payment only against the shipping documents named in the LC. Neither side carries open risk — the standard instrument for first transactions.

· Irrevocable LC from a recognised bank · Documents negotiated through your bank · Best for new relationships and large volumes
TT — WIRE TRANSFER

Telegraphic Transfer

A simple advance-and-balance structure: part payment to confirm the order, balance against scanned shipping documents. Faster and cheaper than an LC.

· Typically 30% advance · 70% against documents · Lower bank charges, quicker turnaround · Preferred by repeat buyers
